Chicken Satay

2 Lbs Chicken Breast
1 Tbsp Ground Coriander
1 Tbsp Curry Powder
1/2 Tsp Turmeric Powder
2 tsp Cumin Powder
1/2 Tsp Pepper
1/2 Cup Coconut Milk
2 Tsp Salt
6-8 Tbsp Sweet Condensed Milk
6 cloves Garlic
1/4 Cup Cilantro
1/4 Cup Lemongrass
Mix together or blend, pour and massage into chicken. Marinate overnight. Cook by air fry, grill, or broil.
Peanut Sauce: Mix peanut butter, Thai red curry paste, coconut milk, Nuoc Cham (Fish Sauce) with warm water, fish sauce, sugar, lime juice, minced garlic, chili. Serve.

Ancho Chicken

1 tbsp ancho chile powder
1 ½ tsp sugar
1 ½ tsp garlic powder
1 ½ tsp cumin
1 ½ tsp black pepper
¾ tsp salt
12 bone-in chicken thighs (~2 ½ lbs)
1 ½ tbsp olive oil
6 tbsp amber agave syrup
3 tbsp tequila
1 ½ tbsp hot sauce
1 ½ tbsp butter
1 ½ tbsp fresh lime juice
½ tsp crushed red pepper
Marinate chicken with first 6 ingredients. Make sauce with remaining, cook chicken, pour sauce over chicken.

Fruit Tart

Crust: 1 stick butter, 1/2 cup sugar, 1/4 tsp salt, 1 egg, 1 tbsp heavy cream, 1 1/2 cups flour.
Filling: 8 oz cream cheese, 1/3 cup sugar, 2 tsp vanilla, 1 cup heavy cream, 2-3 cups berries, 1/4 cup jam.
Make crust, bake, cool. Make filling, pour into crust, top with berries, glaze with warmed apricot preserves.
